About me

Coding Journey

Intrigued by the world of coding, I joined SheCodes in October 2022. From the very beginning, I was fascinated by the possibilities that can be found in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. Whenever something did not work as I imagined, it felt like a puzzle that needed to be solved.
Step by step, the workshops guided me through the world of coding and opened new doors and endless possibilities of what can be created in the coding world. I look forward to continue my learning journey and to create more coding results.

Crafting

Crochet, knitting and sewing are different crafting techniques that I enjoy and explore. With crochet, I created stuffed animals stuch as the giraffe or a baby blanket, that consists of multiple hexagons.
By knitting, I have completed so many pairs of socks and also explored to knit a headband, a woman's cardigan and am currently working on a man's cardigan.
Sewing is the newest craft that I recently added to my portfolio. So far, I have completed a small bag with zipper and a bag. Recently, I also completed a baby towel.

Art & Books

I more or less grew up in a bookstore and then was later on trained as an art historian. So, culture means a lot to me and is my happy place. I travel to see exhibitions and if I can't travel, I use the books to embark on unknown journey and to discover the world some more.
